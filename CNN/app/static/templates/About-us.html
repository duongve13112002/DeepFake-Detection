About Us

Deepfake technology is making it harder to tell whether some news you see and hear on the internet is real or not.

Because it is becoming increasingly common and the enhanced methods of creating Deepfakes are making it more difficult to detect them with the human eye, we decided to contribute to the Deepfake Detection Challenge and provide an open-source model that can detect Deepfakes, whether they are video or images. We provide the model as an online tool for the public to make it easier to verify images and videos circulating on social media.

Please feel free to contact us by email if you find any bugs or/and mistakes in our project.
